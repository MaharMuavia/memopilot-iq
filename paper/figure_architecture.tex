\caption{Architecture and request lifecycle of \osname{}. A user message is
converted into \emph{typed} memories (Extract), embedded and retrieved with a
hybrid dense/sparse signal under structured filters (Retrieve), ranked by the
interpretable linear score of Eq.~\ref{eq:score} (Score), and packed into the
prompt under a token budget that always injects critical/pinned memories
(Assemble). The Qwen LLM produces the answer and a complete \emph{Memory Trace}.
A periodic forgetting sweep expires, archives, and supersedes records through
auditable state transitions; memories and logs persist in the memory/event store
and object store. The same pipeline runs offline (SQLite, deterministic
fallback) and in the cloud (Alibaba Tablestore and OSS, Qwen).}
