\caption{Architecture and request lifecycle of \osname{}. A user message is
converted into \emph{typed} memories (Extract); candidates are embedded and
retrieved with a hybrid dense/sparse signal under structured filters (Retrieve),
ranked by the interpretable linear score of Eq.~\ref{eq:score} (Score), and
packed into the prompt under a strict token budget that always injects
critical/pinned memories (Assemble). The Qwen LLM produces the answer, and a
complete \emph{Memory Trace} records every inclusion and skip decision. A
periodic \emph{forgetting sweep} expires, archives, and supersedes records
through auditable state transitions, while persisted memories and logs live in
the memory/event store and object store. The same pipeline runs offline (SQLite,
deterministic fallback) and in the cloud (Alibaba Tablestore and OSS, Qwen).}
\label{fig:arch}
